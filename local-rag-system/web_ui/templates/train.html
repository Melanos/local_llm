Training Instructions
Documents
  1. Place your .docx files in the data/documents/ folder
  2. Click "Train Documents" to process them
  3. The system will extract text and create searchable chunks
  4. Tables and formatting are preserved
Images
  1. Place your image files in the data/images/ folder
  2. Click "Train Images" to analyze them
  3. AI will generate detailed descriptions
  4. Analysis results are saved and searchable
Note: Training may take several minutes depending on the number and size of files. Image analysis requires GPU resources and may be slower on CPU-only systems.
